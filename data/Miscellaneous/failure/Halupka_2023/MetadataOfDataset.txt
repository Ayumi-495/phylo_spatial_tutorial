Metadata of the dataset (Dataset.tab) to: Halupka et al. "The effect of climate change on avian offspring production: a global meta-analysis"

Dataset.tab is a text-only file, with tab-delimited columns. It consists of 19 columns (variables) and 202 rows. The first row includes variable headings, next follow 201 rows with data, each one describing one population. Missing values are coded as 'NA'.

*******  List of variables with descriptions (see the Method section for details) ************

species: scientific name of the species, names are identical as those used in phylogenetic trees.

slope: change in the production of offspring in local population

slope_SE: standard error of 'slope'
 
mass_f: average mass of an adult female

id_population: unique label for each population

id_species: unique label for each species

broods: binary, 0=single-brooded, 1=multi-brooded

migr2: migratory habits (0=sedentary   1=has long-range seasonal migrations)

meanFED: change in the mean first egg date

meanFED_SE: standard error of 'meanFED'

duration: change in the duration of laying period

duration_SE: standard error of 'duration'

losses: change in the percentage of nests that did not produce fledglings

lossess_SE: standard error of 'losses'

clutch: change in the mean clutch size

clutch_se: standard error of 'clutch'

t1_slope: change in the temperature in the prelaying period

t2_slope: change in the temperature in the laying period

t4_slope: change in the temperature in the nestling period
